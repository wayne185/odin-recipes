Ingredients

  • 1 cup all-purpose flour

  • 1 cup fine ground cornmeal

  • 2/3 cup granulated sugar

  • 1 teaspoon salt

  • 1-1/2 teaspoons baking powder

  • 1/3 cup vegetable oil or melted butter

  • 1 egg

  • 1 cup milk

Steps

  1. Preheat your oven to 400oF

  2. Grease a 9x9 inch baking dish and set aside

  3. In a medium mixing bowl, combine the flour, cornmeal, sugar, salt, and baking powder. Mix thoroughly then make a well in the center to pour the rest of the ingredients into.

  4. Pour in your oil or melted butter, milk, and the egg. Mix until only a few small lumps remain.

  5. Pour the batter into the pan you prepared earlier

  6. Bake 22-25 minutes. Top should be slightly golden and a toothpick should come out clean when inserted into the center.

  7. Serve while hot and enjoy!
